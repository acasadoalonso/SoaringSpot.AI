# 20 Metre Multi-Seat Class Handicap List

**Source:** [SC3AH_2026 — IGC Procedures for Handicapped Classes](https://www.fai.org/sites/default/files/document/file/SC3AH_2026.pdf) (Class D gliders, incl. Class DM motorgliders)
**Edition valid from:** 15 April 2026
**Appendix 2 — IGC 20m Multi-Seat Class List dated:** 1 April 2024

> To be used in conjunction with FAI Sporting Code Section 3, Annex A.

## Eligibility & rules (summary)

- All 20 Metre Multi-seat gliders are eligible. Gliders not appearing in Appendix 2 will normally receive a handicap of **1.00**, subject to IGC Bureau approval.
- **MTOM (§2.5.1):** Takeoff mass must be ≤ the least of the certificated maximum takeoff mass (per TCDS) and **800 kg**.
- **Winglets (§2.6.1):** Addition of winglets does not affect handicaps — winglets are eligible without increase in handicap.
