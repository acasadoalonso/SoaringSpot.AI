## Methodology (FAI Sporting Code §8.5)

- **§8.5.3** Competitor's Team Cup Score = `own day score − class winner's day score + 1000`, for each pilot with a valid launch in a class that had a valid competition day.
- **§8.5.4(a)** Team's Daily Score = mean of its competitors' Team Cup Scores across all classes that had a valid day (rounded to 2 dp). Pilots without a valid launch are excluded.
- **§8.5.5** Team Cup Score = (sum of Team's Daily Scores) ÷ (number of days the team scored), rounded to 2 dp.
- **§8.5.6** Gold / Silver / Bronze awarded to the three highest Team Cup Scores.

## Eligibility and edge cases

- **§8.5.2 (2‑class minimum):** A team must have entries in at least two classes. Excluded as ineligible: ARG, RSA (one class only), and ESP, EST, JAP, NOR, SLO, SWE, USA (18 Metre only). 16 teams qualified.
- **§8.5.4(b)** zero‑score backfill (used only when a team's representation drops below two classes): **never triggered** — every eligible team had a valid launch in ≥2 classes on all 10 days.
- **Non‑launchers** (no valid launch, given no score): 18 Metre day 10 (4 pilots); Open days 3 & 4 (1 pilot each).
- No `not_competing` entries were present in the scored results.
